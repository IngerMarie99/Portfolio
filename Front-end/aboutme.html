About me

Looks matter, but good design is more than that. I find my motivation in creating great user experiences which entice the user to interact out of curiosity, rather than need. I may be analogue in my heart (who doesn’t love the smell of a magazine fresh of the press?), but love to experiment and develop in the ever-changing world of digital media.
