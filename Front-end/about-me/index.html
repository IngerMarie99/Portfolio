Hello there!

I’m Inger Marie, designer.

I hold a bachelor's degree in graphic design, plus a two-year specialisation in UX/UI design from Kristiania.

I nerd out on identity, enjoy editorial, and thrive on delivering great UX that entice people to interact out of curiosity, rather than need. It’s just more fun for everyone.

Currently I’m the in-house designer of Styreplan AS, and run my own company. I’ve been freelancing since I was 16, registering my company as soon as I reached majority, sustaining me through my studies. Rewarded “Golden Sprout” of 2023, I now look forward delivering on that promise.
