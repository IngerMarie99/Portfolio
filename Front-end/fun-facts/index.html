Plants have pores

Plants breathe, and they do so using the pores on the leaves. So make sure you wipe off your dusty plants next time you water them.

Onion-cutting-lifehack

If you stick out your tongue while cutting onions, then your tongue will attract some of the irritating molecules, keeping them from reaching your eyes. That way you won't cry. As a bonus, you will also look incredibly smart while doing it.
